Activity Submission

Greenkeepers USA is a student-led environmental nonprofit trying to reach community based solutions to fight climate change and encourage sustainable practices. Our mission with this website is to encourage people around Fremont to switch to environmentally conscious methods of transportation like biking and walking when possible. When you travel somewhere utilizing a sustainable method of transportation, you can record a submission that can earn you points. These points can later be traded in exchange for discounts, products, and services from partnering local businesses. Help us on our journey to increase healthy, sustainable transportation, and support local businesses along the way!
