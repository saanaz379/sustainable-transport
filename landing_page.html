Greenkeepers Sustainable Travel

Greenkeepers USA is a student-led environmental non-profit aimed at uniting the community to create a sustainable lifestyle. Our mission with this project is to facilitate and incentivize sustainable travel throughout the city of Fremont.

This website will allow users to track trips around the city (in which they utilized a form of sustainable transportation) to earn points. These points can later be traded in exchange for discounts, products, and services from partnering local businesses.
